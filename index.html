ABOUT US

In 1984, Cameron's Seafood first opened its doors to seve the rose city its freshest seafood, and has since become a landmark in Pasadena. Cameron's brings the ocean feeling to you with its San Francisco style fish house, surrounded by its photos of fishing history reminiscent of the old fishing days. Its exhibition style kitchen allows patrons to watch their fresh catch being cooked over its mesquite wood grills that burn all day long. Cameron's Fish Market serves fresh fish from morning until night. From shrimp platters to halibut filets, or Maine Lobster. Cameron's maintains the finest selection of fresh seafood from all parts of the globe. Our friendly bar allows patrons to enjoy a good meal and even one of our famous martinis while watching your favorite sporting event. For the past 27 years, Cameron's has won many awards for the best seafood in the Pasadena Weekly, The Arcadia Weekly, The Mountain Views, and recently Best Seafood from the Boulevard Magazine. We have achieved our success because we base it on our commitment to offering our guests a great value without sacrificing quality, consistenc and most of all, great service.

PRIVATE DINNING

 

Cameron's Seafood Restaurant has two (2) banquet rooms for small and large parties and plenty of free parking.  Our small room provides seating for 25 people and our large room has a capacity for 50-70 people. There is no charge for renting of the entire room.

 

Whether you are planning a business gathering, a birthday party, or any other private function,

we can accommodate you with a personalized menu and affordable group pricing.
